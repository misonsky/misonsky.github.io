I am an Assistant Professor at Northeastern University, where I earned my Ph.D. under the supervision of Professors Wang Daling and Feng Shi. My doctoral research focused on dialogue systems, multilingual NLP, and LLM fine-tuning. From 2022 to 2024, I am a joint Ph.D. student at LMU Munich, advised by ACL Fellow Professor Hinrich Schütze. 

Undergraduate and Master's students interested in LLMs and NLP are welcome to contact me.
